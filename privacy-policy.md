# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 9, 2026

## Privacy Policy for LoreKeeper

Thank you for using LoreKeeper.

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how LoreKeeper handles your information.

### Information We Collect

LoreKeeper does **not collect, store, or share any personal information**.

The app does not require you to create an account and does not collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location
- Contacts
- Photos
- Financial information
- Health information
- Browsing history
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics

### Local Storage

LoreKeeper stores your library, reading progress, ratings, and preferences **locally on your device** using Flutter Hive database.

This information never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up yourself using your own methods.

### Third-Party Services

LoreKeeper uses the Google Books API only to search and display publicly available book information.

No personal information is sent to Google through LoreKeeper.

Google Books API:
https://developers.google.com/books

### Analytics

LoreKeeper does not use analytics services.

### Advertising

LoreKeeper does not display advertisements.

### Tracking

LoreKeeper does not track users across apps or websites.

### Children's Privacy

LoreKeeper does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
