# 📖 About

**LoreKeeper** is a premium reading companion designed for readers who love books, authors, and fictional universes.

Instead of being another social reading app, LoreKeeper focuses on creating the most beautiful and intuitive personal library experience.

Whether you're reading **Mistborn**, **The Stormlight Archive**, **Red Rising**, or **The Wheel of Time**, LoreKeeper helps you organize your reading journey.
