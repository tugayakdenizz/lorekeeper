LoreKeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Overview

LoreKeeper is a reading companion app designed to help users organize their personal library, track reading progress, rate books, and manage reading goals.

Information We Collect

LoreKeeper does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. The app does not require an account and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, contacts, photos, financial information, health information, device identifiers, or usage analytics.

Local Storage

Reading progress, library data, ratings, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This information is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

LoreKeeper may use the Google Books API to search and display publicly available book information such as titles, authors, descriptions, and cover images. LoreKeeper does not send personal user information to Google.

Analytics and Advertising

LoreKeeper does not use analytics services and does not display advertisements.

Tracking

LoreKeeper does not track users across apps or websites.

Children’s Privacy

LoreKeeper does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be published on this page.
